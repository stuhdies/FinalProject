About Us

Here at Austin's Campground, we are dedicated to providing a relaxing experience. We all need a good break from reality and the best way to clear your mind, is in nature. We are here to help you relax and escape with multiple activities and attractions. With us you will never have wonder "what can I do?", as we offer the best options for the explorer in you! From Kayaking, fishing, canoeing, and hiking, we have you covered! Don't have everything you need? Thats okay, we have kayaks and canoes for rent for the duration of your stay. We also have on hand fishing poles if you need one too! We offer campsites for rent for both tents and campers. We aslo have multiple cabins for low rates. We have onsite restrooms and showers: you can purchase toiletries at our welcome desk. For more information, please feel free to give us a call!
