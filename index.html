Meet Mother Nature

Take a break from life and explore your wild side.

Our Purpose

Looking for a peaceful get away? Well, you've stumbled to the right place. Our mission is to be your home away from home, providing you with peace from everyday life. We all deserve a good break from time to time, and here at Austin's Campground, we believe in a good break. We have plenty of activities for you and the whole family to enjoy! We have a beautiful lake and a great trail for hiking or if you enjoy exercise, take a jog on the trail. Come experience the great outdoors and get a breath of fresh air!

Information

  • Kayaking

    Come fuel your brain with good vibes. Kayaking is a great way to relieve stress and give your body some good exercise.

  • Fishing

    Get connected with nature by fishing in our lake. Fishing helps you focus and keeps you calm, and being in the sun helps build your immune system.

  • Canoeing

    Looking for an exciting group activity? Look no further as we have instructor based canoeing or you can canoe with your friends.

  • Jogging

    Why not start your morning off with a jog around our beautiful park. The trails are marked with orange tape to make sure you dont get lost.
